    Inventory methods designed to permit rapid, replicable, and quantitative sampling of the leaf
    litter ant fauna were used to investigate altitudinal and latitudinal patterns in ant diversity in
    wet tropical forests in eastern Madagascar. A total of 14 inventories were conducted at four
    regional sites. I discuss the nature of leaf litter ant assemblages in Madagascar and present
